Our Keynote Speakers

Dr. Rohit Bhakar

Associate Professor, MNIT Jaipur

  • Ph.D.
  • M. Tech.
  • B.E.

Dr. Bhakar, with a research experience from IIT Roorkee and University of Bath, UK, leads the Power System Management group, performing research in Power System Operation and Planning, Electricity Markets, Risk Management, Network Pricing, Forecasting, Smart Grid Management, Frequency Responses, Ancillary Services & Energy Storage. He has held multiple research grants from Indian and international funding agencies, including two India-UK Consortium grants, in association with several IITs and UK universities. He is Secretary to IEEE PES working group on Network Charging and a Senior Member of IEEE.
